Biography

你好，我的名字叫邱丰羽。

I have joined Morgan Stanley as a full-time Analyst in the Technology Division in August 2019.

I have received my Master’s Degree in Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University in June 2019. My supervisor is professor Dai Xinyu, in the group of Natural Language Processing, in Department of Computer Science and Technology. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University in June 2016.

Experience

  • 2019.08-current, Technology Analyst Program in Morgan Stanley IT, Shanghai.
  • 2018.07-2018.09, Technology Summer Analyst (Intern), Morgan Stanley, Shanghai.
  • Teaching Assistant of Compilers: Principles, Techniques, and Tools. ( For undergraduate students, in Fall 2018 and Spring 2017 )

Interests

I am interested in cutting-edge computer science and technologies. For a better life, I like running, cooking, listening to music and doing some small but meaningful things.
